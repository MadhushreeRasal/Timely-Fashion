Description:

Inspired by the Millenia family, this eye-catching bracelet watch offers color pop appeal. Expertly crafted from a row of octagon-cut crystals in a vibrant blue hue, it features a blue dial, a crystallized bezel, and a stainless steel finish. It is water resistant to 50m and makes a playful choice for solo styling or layering.
